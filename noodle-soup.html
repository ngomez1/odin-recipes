Noodle Soup

Description

Noodle Soup is a simple dish made from chicken broth and noodles.

The recipe below will provide enough Noodle Soup for two adults.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. noodles
  • 3 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cup water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 3 cups of chicken broth and 2 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add salt to the water and dump in the noodles
  3. Boil for 10 minutes, add pepper to taste, and serve
