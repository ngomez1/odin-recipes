Penne Vodka

Description

Penne Vodka is a simple dish made from pasta, tomato paste, cream, cheese, and a few aromatics.

The recipe below will provide enough Penne Vodka for two adults.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. penne
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/8 cup vodka
  • 1 medium shallot
  • 6 oz of tomato paste
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • salt and red pepper flakes to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add salt to the water and dump in the penne
  3. Dice the garlic and shallots and add to a pan with 2 tablespoons of butter on medium heat
  4. Sweat the aromatics in the butter for 5 minutes, and then add the tomato paste and red pepper flake to taste
  5. After five minutes, add the vodka, cream, and cheese and mix thoroughly
  6. After the penne has boiled for 10 minutes, remove from pot and strain
  7. Combine the sauce with the penne and serve
